About

Maya Chen

Writer, thinker, obsessive reader.

I write about the things I can't stop thinking about — technology and its discontents, the philosophy of everyday life, what science tells us about being human, and the occasional deeply personal essay that I'm not sure I should publish.

By day I'm a UX researcher at a tech company in San Francisco. By night I'm here, filling margins with ideas that don't quite fit anywhere else.

This blog started as a private notebook in 2019. I made it public because a friend dared me to. I'm glad she did.
